Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about ReportAuto

What data sources does ReportAuto support? +

We support 100+ integrations including 1C:Enterprise, Ozon, Wildberries, Yandex.Market, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, Stripe, QuickBooks, and custom APIs. Perfect for Russia & CIS markets. If your data source isn't listed, our API can connect almost anything.

How secure is my data? +

We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification, use end-to-end encryption, and store all data in AWS with multi-region redundancy. Your data is never shared with third parties and you maintain full control.

Can I customize the report design? +

Yes, completely! Our visual builder lets you customize fonts, colors, logos, layouts, and more. You can also embed custom CSS for advanced styling and create branded templates for your organization.

What's the typical setup time? +

Most customers have their first automated report running within 30-60 minutes. Our onboarding team can guide you through setup, and we offer pre-built templates for common report types to get you started faster.
